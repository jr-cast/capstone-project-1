OPEN BIM GLOBAL DEVELOPERS SUMMIT 2022

The Open BIM Global Summit brings togueter the community of experts, researchers, and practitioners who comprise the Open BIM affiliate network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals interested in applying open BIM tools in an effort to make BIM models more accessible to everyone and not dependant on authoring BIM tools.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about Open BIM Global Summit 2022!

openBIM2022@openbim.org
