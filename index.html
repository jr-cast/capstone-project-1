"Open BIM tools for everyone!"

OPEN BIM GLOBAL DEVELOPERS SUMMIT 2022

A joyful celebration believing in the value of open bim tools and shared workflows, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries.

2022.12.02 (FRI) ~ 03 (SAT)

@Camino Real Polanco Conference Center CDMX

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the speakers from various contries about the messages of open BIM

Demos

Appreciate recent open BIM developments that enable the creation of open tools

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with open BIM and AECO experts

Network

Try creating your own open BIM tool using available resources such as IFCjs

Job fair

Get to network and meet people from AECO indutry and look for job offers

SEE THE WHOLE PROGRAM

Feature Speakers

Antonio Gonzalez

Web developer and one of the creators of IFC.js

Creator of IFCjs, an open source effort to decentralize the design and development of BIM models from authorative tools

Dimitrie Stefanescu

Architect and founder of Speckle.

Accidentally gave birth to Speckle while pursuing his PhD at UCL. Speckle put you in charge of your design and construction data.

Dorothy Williams

IFC developer and collaborator at buildingsmart

Dorothy is currently in charge of the development of the new and improved IFC schema which is focusing on enhacing interoperability and avoid spaguetti code.

Marcus Stephenson

Virtual designer and AI expert

Marcus is an expert on applying AI for building design purposes, from enhanced early design aspects to using real time data from building an feed AI models for retrofitting insight.

Johana Moore

Leader system integrator at ARUP

Johana is the main system integrator at ARUP in london. She will be talking about novel approaches when handling multiple and very large BIM models

Hirata Motoko

UCL researcher at Faculty of Built Environment

Hirata will demostrate a new approach to display BIM models in real time. His research focuses on virtualization of physical assets en augmented reality
